An engaging and inclusive photo-taking experience

When browsing through old photos, our memories and emotional connections are strongest when seeing photos that include ourselves. But documenting one's own presence is not an easy task. One person usually has to be excluded from photos taken at social events. ‘DUO’ allows you to be photographer and subject at the same time. It's a separable camera with synchronised triggers. By inviting people to engage in the photo-taking process, DUO allows you to have fun documenting while being documented.
